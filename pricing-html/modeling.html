Modeling

Baseline of 3h of photography (+$100/hour after) and a limit of 7 poses. However, the client can take unlimited amounts of photos for each of the 7 poses within the time limit. "Headshots" and the "Torso Up" are only for basic modeling portofolio needs and not specialized photography.

Headshots - $250

  • Photos from the neck up
  • Basic Shots w/ Blank Background
  • Includes 7 physical copies of images

Torso Up - $350

  • Photos from the torso up
  • Basic shots w/ Blank Backgrounds
  • Includes 10 physical copies of images

Full Body - $425

  • Full body images w/ freedom of 7 different poses
  • Can be specialized photography
  • Includes 15 physical copies of images

The "Headshot" and "Torso Up" packages must be done in my studio as the lighting equipment and background are necessary. Included in every package is a digital version of the photos with printing rights that is given in the form of a USB stick. Clients can also be emailed the photos free of cost. The prices also include editing and touchpups at the clients' request within reason.
