About Xavier Len

Born in Alberta, Canada, Xavier Len fell in love with the beautiful scenery of nature as a young child. This love for scenery prompted him to pursue the field of photography as it can capture the scenery from a new perspective. After finishing college, Xavier decided to move to California as many well-known influencers live there. Compared to his hometown of Alberta, it was different since the entire state is nearly urbanized. Alongside the difference, Xavier also noticed another thing; he noticed everything in California is so similar and everyone wanted to get that perfect photo or get the newest thing, which did not show much originality. After acknowledging the flaw of the cities, he fell in love with a type of contemporary photography known as Wabi-Sabi, which places importance on nature and imperfections. These ideas are not well-known in the state that he currently lives in as perfection is the theme of certain photos. Wabi-Sabi teaches those to find beauty and appreciation in what is natural and what is imperfect, for those have a uniqueness that could not be found in something perfect.
