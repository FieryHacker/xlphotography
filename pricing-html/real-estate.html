Real Estate

All packages must be done on a sunny day in the afternoon in order to capture the best photos
Note: Prices may vary depending on the size of the property

Basic Package - $300

  • One physical photo for each room of the house
  • Up to 35 digital images
  • Include exterior and interior photos

Advanced Package - $500+

  • Three physical photos for each room of the real estate
  • Up to 50 digital images
  • Includes exterior and interior photos

Aerial Videotography - $350

  • 2-minute video showcasing the property's exterior
  • Custom editing w/ client's music of choice

If the weather suddenly changes on the day of the appointment, the appointment day can be changed free of charge to a later time. All packages include a digital version of the photos taken with printing rights that are given in the form of a USB stick and can also be emailed free of cost. The prices also include editing and any touch-ups the clients request within reason.
